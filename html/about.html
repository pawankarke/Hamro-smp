Welcome to Hamro SMP

About Hamro_SMP?

"Hamro SMP" is a Minecraft server that beckons players into a vibrant virtual world where creativity knows no bounds. The name "Hamro," derived from Nepali, meaning "ours," sets the tone for a communal gaming experience. Within this SMP (Survival Multiplayer) server, players embark on a shared journey, forging alliances, constructing elaborate structures, and navigating the challenges of survival together. Hamro SMP is more than just a server; it's a digital canvas where individuals come together to shape their unique narratives, crafting a collaborative tapestry of adventures in the pixelated realms of Minecraft. Join Hamro SMP, where the spirit of community and boundless imagination converge in the enchanting landscape of the blocky universe.

Who created Minecraft?

Minecraft was created by Markus Persson, also known as Notch. He initially developed the game alone, but it quickly gained popularity and he formed a company called Mojang Studios to continue its development. In 2014, Microsoft acquired Mojang for $2.5 billion.
